About me

Hello! I'm Yana, a young UX/UI designer who is passionate about making user interfaces that are both aesthetically pleasing and intuitive.

Studying graphic design gave me a solid foundation in colour, typography, and composition, sparking my enthusiasm for design. This background has honed my eye for detail and strong sense of visual design. Transitioning into UX/UI, I learned the importance of user experience in creating digital products.

As a UX/UI designer, I have developed proficiency in tools like Adobe XD and Figma, using them to bring my ideas to life. I am skilled in user research, wireframing, and prototyping, ensuring my designs are both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly. I am dedicated to lifelong learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends and best practices.

I believe in the value of collaboration and teamwork, working closely with cross-functional teams to solve complex problems elegantly and effectively, satisfying both users and business goals.
