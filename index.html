About me

Hi! I'm Yana, a UX/UI designer dedicated to creating interfaces that are both visually appealing and user-friendly.

After earning the prestigious International Diplomatic Scholarship, which granted me a full four-year education at a Korean university (awarded to only two individuals per nationality), I spent six years in Korea immersing myself in Eastern design perspectives. This unique experience shaped my approach to design, blending cultural nuance with global usability standards.

Now based in Europe, I have broadened my professional horizons and gained a cross-cultural understanding of UX/UI. I'm committed to continuous learning and excited by new challenges to stay ahead in the field.

I value collaboration and strive to create solutions that benefit users and achieve business goals alike.
